Europe

Europe full of beautiful countries with lots of history. The countries are often very different both culturally and aesthetically. It has been a very popular travel destination for many years.

Italy

Italy has always been a very popular travel spot. There are many different places to visit in Italy because of how diverse it is. There are a lot of sightseeing opportunities, hikes, beaches and shopping to do all over Italy. Below is a suggested place to visit while in Italy.

Rome

Price of activity: $$$$$
Name of hotel/hostel:Alessandro Downtown Hostel
price of Hotel/hostel: $$$
Safety of location: Safe, however there are many pickpockets in Rome.

Notes: Although it is very expensive to visit Rome, there are many activities to do while visiting. It is suggested to buy a pass for a 'hop-on hop-off bus'. The bus will take you around the city to popular sightseeing destinations such as the Colosseum, the Trevi fountain, the Vatican and more. The bus will also give information on the city while riding.


Slovenia


Slovenia is a very small European country. It is arguably one of the most beautiful countries in the world. It is a mountainous area with lush green hills. there are many physical activities to do in Slovenia. Below is a suggested place to visit while in Slovenia.

Kranjska Gora

Price of activity: $
Name of hotel/hostel: Hostel Nika
price of Hotel/hostel: $$
Safety of location: Very safe

Notes: Kranjska Gora is a small town in Slovenia located in the Alpes. In the winter, it is a world class skiing town. However, in the summer, there are many activities to do such as mountain biking, hiking and kayaking.


Scotland


Scotland is a beautiful country. it has been a very popular travel destination for years. It has lots of history which allows individuals to do lots of sightseeing as well as lots of hiking and nightlife activites. Below is a suggested activity to do while in Scotland.

Visit Edinburgh

Price of activity: $$
Name of hotel/hostel: Castle Rock Hostel
price of Hotel/hostel: $$
Safety of location: Very safe.

Notes: Edinburgh is a beautiful city with lots to do. There are a lot of old buildings to sightsee as well as a hike to 'Arthur's Seat' which is a large hill that overlooks the city. The nightlife in the town is also very fun and reasonably priced.
