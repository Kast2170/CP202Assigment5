About Budget Backpacking

Budget Backpacking is a website that was created to help individuals plan their dream backpacking adventures.

The information given by Budget Backpacking has been collected from research all over the internet as well as from surveys that were filled out by individuals that have been on backpacking trips. At Budget Backpacking, we feel that the best information comes from experiences. This allows us to process the information and give tips about the locations visited.
